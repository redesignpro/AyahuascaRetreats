Tips

Tip for responding to a positive review

You could use this opportunity to thank the reviewer. Please make sure to personalize your response and avoid using standard or short replies like ‘Thank you’, since these may come across as insincere.

Tip for responding to a bad review

Try to convey that you deeply understand how the customer feels, even if you didn’t cause whatever made them upset. You should also try to understand and resolve the issue quickly and get to the bottom of why the mistake occurred, without being defensive or blaming anyone; focus on fixing the process so that it doesn’t happen again.
Do keep in mind that your reply will be seen by all the customers that visit your listings and business page so please try to follow these best practices for how you should format your reply:
  • Use polite and pleasant language.
  • Respect the privacy of the customer.
  • Be professional and use standard formatting.
  • Don't hastily reply, think carefully about how you want to explain your side of the story.
Note: Please be notified that we do not allow and will remove any contact details that you share in your reply.
