Tips on videos

To maintain the quality of the Tripaneer website, videos will be reviewed by Tripaneer before they are displayed on your page.

Best videos to upload:

  • No external links (eg. www.mysite.com) or contact information
  • Short videos, up to 7 minutes long
  • Real videos instead of picture slideshows
  • Up to 50MB (If your video is bigger, you can use Handbrake or MPEG Streamclip to make it smaller)
  • No inappropriate content (foul language, nudity, sexual activities, platform banned activities like hunting, elephant riding, fishing etc.)

Suggested content and quality:

  • Quality: 1080p is preferred, but not mandatory
  • Show the activities, accommodations, and surroundings
  • Introduce the instructors, hosts or staff
  • Testimonials from guests describing what they love about your theme vacation

As an example, have a look at the following videos: Salty Pelican

Why are high quality photos and videos important?

In Tripaneer’s user research, guests have told us that high-quality images are crucial to their booking decision. They even told us that it’s their most important factor in deciding to book a retreat, and use the photos and videos to determine which listings are interesting for them to consider. The listings with professional photos got significantly more inquiries and bookings than listings without.
